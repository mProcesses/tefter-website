<section class="section">
  <div class="wrapper section-center">
    <h1 class="section-title">Partner Edge — a zero‑integration, white‑label partner portal</h1>
    <p class="section-copy">
      Partner Edge is a lightweight endpoint you can run under your own domain. It lets non-technical partners log in and transact with you immediately — without EDI setup, without custom integrations, and without waiting on IT.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <div class="wrapper">
    <h2 class="section-title">What it is (and why it matters)</h2>
    <div class="card-grid">
      <div class="card">
        <h3>Zero-integration endpoint</h3>
        <p>Bring the long tail of suppliers online without integration projects or partner tooling.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <h3>Faster partner adoption</h3>
        <p>More partners can transact sooner — so procurement isn’t blocked by integrations, and long-tail onboarding becomes simple and scalable.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <h3>Onboarding accelerator</h3>
        <p>Start with a portal today, then move to deeper EDI/API integration only where it pays off.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
