### TX FEATURES IN DEPTH

**Format-Agnostic Exchange**

TX supports any data format without custom development:
- **EDI:** X12, EDIFACT, VDA, all standard transaction sets
- **XML:** Industry standards (UBL, GS1) and custom schemas
- **JSON:** RESTful APIs and custom structures
- **CSV:** Spreadsheet-based interchange
- **Email:** PDF attachments, structured text
- **Custom Protocols:** Proprietary formats via configuration

**How It Works:**
1. Partner sends data in their format
2. TX receives and validates structure
3. TX transforms to internal canonical model
4. TX routes to destination in their required format
5. Confirmations flow back automatically

**Business Value:** Onboard any partner regardless of their technical capabilities. No "you must use our format" restrictions. Support retail giants (complex EDI) and small suppliers (email) with one platform.

---

**Automatic Retry and Recovery ‚Äî Deep Dive**

**Network Failure Recovery:**
- TX monitors all connections with heartbeat checks
- Failure detected within 10 seconds
- Messages queued with encrypted persistence
- Retry strategy: exponential backoff (30 sec, 1 min, 2 min, 5 min, 15 min, then hourly)
- When connection restored, queued messages sent in order
- No message loss, guaranteed delivery

**Partner System Outage:**
- TX distinguishes between network issues and partner system failures
- Notifications sent to your team and partner team
- Automatic resume when partner system available
- Historical data: TX has maintained 99.9% uptime across partner outages

**Data Center Failure:**
- Multi-zone deployment across GCP Brussels region
- Automatic failover to secondary zone within 60 seconds
- No data loss (synchronous replication)
- RTO (Recovery Time Objective): <5 minutes
- RPO (Recovery Point Objective): Zero (no data loss)
